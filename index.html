About

War of Dots: Last stand is a game created by a single developer: TempFibre.
The developement started around March 2025, with updates being released every few weeks.
The game's main objective is to fight of an enemy army and capture their flag. This can be done with good strategy and critical thinking. You can buy and arange units before the battle starts to have the highest chances of winning.
